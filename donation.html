Program Impacts

Our various programmes help us reach out to those in need and provide for their safety and well-being.

Grace Food Bank

Serving around 100-150 people every day at a price of Rs. 10, whereas the actual cost comes around Rs. 30 per meal which includes rent and salaries.

The Mission Smile Program

Provides 100% free cleft repair surgeries and comprehensive care for children worldwide. We empower local medical professionals with the skills and resources to deliver ongoing cleft care.

Disability Elimination Program

Led by the Real Happiness of Life Foundation (RHLF), it is focused on early detection, treatment, and rehabilitation of children with birth defects and developmental delays.
